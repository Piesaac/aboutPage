About us

The Creative Coding Collective is the first cohort of the Creative Coding Porgram ran by Maiona Coding and The Albany. We have come together on this project to share what we have learnt so far and also encourage those who are interested in coding and tech to join our coding community, while also giving future participants a platform to express their creativity.
